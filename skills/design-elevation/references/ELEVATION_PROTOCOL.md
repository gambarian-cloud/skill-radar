# Elevation Protocol

Use this loop after the first functional version exists.

1. Foundation check
- Confirm the output works.
- Fix structural or usability problems before polishing.

2. Diagnose the weakness
- Name the real problem: generic, cluttered, flat, weak hierarchy, timid, inconsistent, poor readability, weak states.

3. Choose direction
- Pick one primary visual direction from `REFERENCE_LIBRARY.md`.

4. Choose techniques
- Pick 1 or 2 strong techniques from `TECHNIQUE_CATALOG.md`.

5. Refine the whole system
- Improve typography, spacing, color, composition, and states together.
- Do not polish one area while leaving the rest generic.

6. Apply restraint
- Remove any move that is flashy but not useful.
- Keep the strongest ideas and cut the rest.

7. Interrogate before shipping
- Run `DESIGN_INTERROGATION.md`.

8. Compare before and after
- Be explicit about what changed and why the design is stronger.
